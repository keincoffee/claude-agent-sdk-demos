1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
--------------------
This document specifies the FY2025 sales planning cycle for Nexus Analytics Inc.,
a B2B analytics software company. The planning cycle covers territory design, quota
setting, compensation plan mechanics, and workflow configuration for implementation
in Varicent Sales Performance Management (SPM).

Company FY2025 Revenue Target: $18,000,000 New ARR
Number of Quota-Carrying Reps: 8 (plus 1 ramping rep excluded from full quota)
Planning Cycle Start: February 1, 2025
Go-Live Date: January 15, 2025

Key Design Principles:
  - Pay-for-performance with clear line-of-sight between effort and reward
  - Territory balance: no rep should carry more than 2x another rep's addressable market
  - Quota fairness: methodology must be explainable to reps in plain language
  - Simple accelerator structure (max 2 tiers above quota)

3.5 Prospect vs. Customer Split

  Target per rep: minimum 40% of accounts should be prospects (growth pipeline)
  Customer accounts provide renewal and expansion revenue (not counted in new ARR quota)


4. QUOTA METHODOLOGY
---------------------
4.1 Top-Down Target

  Company FY2025 New ARR Target: $18,000,000
  Regional allocation:
    East Region:    $5,400,000   (30% of total)
    West Region:    $8,100,000   (45% of total)
    Central Region: $4,500,000   (25% of total)

4.2 Distribution Formula

  Individual quotas are calculated using a weighted formula:
    Rep Quota = Total Regional Target × Rep Share

    Rep Share = (0.60 × Historical Component) + (0.40 × Market Potential Component)

  Historical Component:
    - Average of FY2023 and FY2024 annual attainment percentages (for reps with 2 years)
    - FY2024 only for reps with < 2 full years (David Kim)
    - Ramp discount applies for reps with < 12 months tenure (see Section 4.4)
    - Normalize across reps within the same region so components sum to 1.0

  Market Potential Component:
    - Sum of annual_revenue_millions for assigned prospect accounts × 0.02
      (assumed 2% win rate on new logos)
    - Plus sum of current_arr_usd for assigned customer accounts × 0.15
      (assumed 15% expansion rate)
    - Normalize across reps within the same region so components sum to 1.0

4.3 Quota Floors and Ceilings

  No individual quota may exceed 30% of the regional target
  No individual quota may be less than $1,200,000 (floor for AE role)
  No individual quota may be less than $1,800,000 (floor for Senior AE role)
  Enterprise AE floor: $3,000,000

4.4 Ramp Schedule

  Reps with < 6 months tenure at plan start (Feb 1, 2025) receive a ramp discount:
    Tom Anderson (3 months tenure): 40% ramp quota (full ramp by Q3 2025)

  Ramping rep quota counts toward regional total but at discounted rate.
  Ramp schedule: Q1=20%, Q2=30%, Q3=25%, Q4=25% of annual ramped quota

4.5 Quarterly Seasonality

  Full quota reps — standard quarterly distribution:
    Q1 (Feb–Apr):  22% of annual
    Q2 (May–Jul):  25% of annual
    Q3 (Aug–Oct):  25% of annual
    Q4 (Nov–Jan):  28% of annual

  Note: FY2025 runs February 1, 2025 – January 31, 2026


5. COMPENSATION PLAN MECHANICS
--------------------------------
5.1 Pay Mix

  All roles: 50/50 base salary to variable compensation (OTE = base × 2)
  Variable is earned as commission on new ARR closed.

5.2 Commission Rates (On-Target)

  Commission rate at 100% attainment = 50% of OTE ÷ Annual Quota
  Example: Sarah Chen OTE $200K → variable $100K ÷ $2.25M quota = 4.44% commission rate

5.3 Accelerator Structure

  Tier 1 (100%–120% attainment): 1.5× base commission rate
  Tier 2 (>120% attainment):     2.0× base commission rate

  Accelerators apply to the incremental deals above threshold, not retroactively.

5.4 Draw Policy

  No recoverable draw. Base salary is guaranteed.
  Commission accrues monthly based on closed-won deals.

5.5 Clawback Policy

  Deals cancelled within 90 days of close are subject to commission clawback.
  Deals >$500K require VP approval before commission is paid.


6. APPROVAL WORKFLOW
---------------------
6.1 Quota Approval Chain

  Step 1: Rep acknowledges quota letter (digital signature required)
           Deadline: February 15, 2025
  Step 2: Regional VP approves territory assignments and quotas
           Deadline: January 20, 2025
  Step 3: CRO final approval of full plan
           Deadline: January 31, 2025
  Step 4: HR records comp plan in system of record
           Deadline: February 1, 2025 (plan go-live)

6.2 Dispute Resolution

  Reps may dispute their quota within 10 business days of receiving quota letter.
  Disputes escalate: Rep → Manager → VP → Compensation Committee
  Compensation Committee meets monthly; decisions are final.

6.3 Mid-Year Adjustments

  Territory changes require VP + CRO approval.
  Quota adjustments permitted only for: role change, leave of absence > 30 days,
  or company reorganization.


7. VARICENT CONFIGURATION REQUIREMENTS
----------------------------------------
7.1 Data Tables Required

  REPS table: rep_id, rep_name, title, region, email, manager_name,
              hire_date, ote_usd, base_salary_usd, tenure_months

  ACCOUNTS table: account_id, account_name, industry, hq_state, region,
                  annual_revenue_millions, segment, current_arr_usd, account_status

  TERRITORY_ASSIGNMENTS table: account_id, rep_id, effective_date, rationale

  QUOTA_TARGETS table: rep_id, period (Q1/Q2/Q3/Q4/Annual),
                       quota_amount, quota_type (new_arr)

  PERFORMANCE table: rep_id, period, actual_new_arr, quota_amount,
                     attainment_pct, commission_earned

7.2 Import Specifications

  All monetary values in USD (whole numbers, no decimals)
  All percentages as decimal (e.g., 112.5% → 112.5)
  Dates in ISO 8601 format (YYYY-MM-DD)
  Encoding: UTF-8
  Delimiter: comma (,)
  Header row required on all files

7.3 Calculation Order in Varicent

  1. Load REPS and ACCOUNTS
  2. Load TERRITORY_ASSIGNMENTS
  3. Calculate QUOTA_TARGETS (from formula in Section 4)
  4. Load PERFORMANCE data (monthly actuals feed)
  5. Calculate commissions from PERFORMANCE vs QUOTA_TARGETS

7.4 Key Reports Required

  - Territory coverage map (accounts per rep, revenue potential per rep)
  - Quota attainment dashboard (MTD, QTD, YTD by rep and region)
  - Pipeline-to-quota ratio by rep (minimum 3× coverage expected)
  - Commission accrual report (monthly, for payroll)
  - Accelerator earned report (quarterly)
